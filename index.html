Odyssey Gaming

Odyssey can trace its history back to 2011 when DC Universe Online launched. There, a group of players decided they wanted more out of their gaming experience. Their unifying goals? Be the best players they can, dominate content, and find ways to give back to the community.

Our History

Worldwide

The league, once a core group of players based in Europe and playing DCUO, is now a spread across multiple continents and has enjoyed numerous games together.

Improvement

We strongly believe in being at the forefront of content — mechanics, tactics and/or loadouts — so we can actively help ourselves and fellow players excel.

Community

We have produced numerous guides, commentaries, updates, tips, videos, and illustrations and were even immortalized in DCUO by having a feat named after us as a consequence of our contributions.

Discord

We utilize a private Discord to keep in touch with league mates.

Our Games

Our members can be found enjoying a wide-range of games and genres. Our main focus in grouped games include:

MMORPG
DC Universe Online

DCUO was the birthplace of Odyssey. This is where Odyssey members first strived to better both themselves and their game's community. The league was known for their written and video guides, CR Calculator, and community events.

MMOFPS
Destiny

Our Destiny clan began began as a group of our core members from Europe playing together on PS4 during the first game. It now includes players on multiple consoles in both Europe and the US. To join us in Destiny 2, please apply to our Bungie Clan Site.

Co-Op
Borderlands

The Borderlands series has seen serious game time for many within the Odyssey community. From the original game up to the newest release, the mix of comedy with some good old-fashioned FPS has appealed to numerous members.
