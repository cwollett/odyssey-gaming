DCUO Combat Rating Calculator

This Combat Rating (CR) Calculator has been developed as a tool for DC Universe Online players to input item levels and generate overall CR.

How does it work?

Overall CR is calculated with the highest level gear for that slot regardless of whether it is equipped, in your inventory, or in your bank. Utility belts and trinkets occupy the same slot so you should enter whichever is highest, even if the trinket is equipped in a utility belt slot.

The game also now displays a currently equipped CR which only uses the information of gear your character is currently wearing.

Which piece of vendor gear should I buy?

While leveling, players will want to buy gear that gives them the highest CR increase for the lowest marks. After filling in the item levels of your gear above, enter the item level of the vendor gear you are considering. The "Purchase" box will display what you should buy.

Note: Use at your own risk. A number of assumptions are being taken here. Vendor costs are based off source mark costs as of 2021. The calculation assumes you have put your highest possible gear in every slot, including trinket, which should be the highest out of your trinkets and utility belt options.
